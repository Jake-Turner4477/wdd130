Dry Oar River Rafting Co.

At Dry Oar River Rafting, we value safety, nature, and adventure—guiding every journey with integrity and wild-hearted spirits.

History

Dry Oar River Rafting Co. was founded in 1992 with just two rafts and a dream of not drowning alone. What began as a small family business on the Colorado River has grown into a trusted name in white water rafting.

Today, we guide trips on some of the most scenic rivers in North America—always with a focus on safety, fun, and a deep respect for nature. At Dry Oar, the adventure begins the moment you hit the water.
