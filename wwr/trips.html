Choose Your Adventure!

From calm scenic floats to adrenaline-pumping rapids, we offer white water rafting trips for all experience levels. Led by expert guides, each trip combines excitement, safety, and stunning natural views. Explore below to find the perfect adventure for you!

Devils Narrow

Glide through calm waters with just enough splashes to keep you smiling. Devil's Narrow offers gentle Class I-II rapids, perfect for families,first-time rafters, and anyone who wants to take in the breathtaking canyon scenery at a relaxed pace. No experience needed — just bring your sense of adventure.

Thunder Run

Ride the rhythm of the river as you take on rolling Class III rapids that mix excitement with manageable challenges. Thunder Run is ideal for those with a bit of paddling experience or thrill-seekers who want more action without going overboard. Expect splashes, laughs, and a healthy dose of adrenaline.

Widowmaker Gorge

Not for the faint of heart. Widowmaker Gorge hurls you into pounding Class IV-V rapids, towering waves, and heart-pounding drops through a narrow, rock-lined chasm. This expedition demands strong teamwork, quick reflexes, and a taste for pure chaos. You'll come out drenched, exhausted — and grinning ear to ear.
